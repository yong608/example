CLUSTER® Drum and Bugle Corps Association is an organization comprising different drum and bugle corps in the islands of Cebu and the neighboring regions that aims to preserve the drum and bugle methods of Cebu, promote camaraderie and discipline in order to regain the wholesome impression from the public while encouraging colleagues to accept change and evolve into better individuals so as to become a prototype of a Tambolista De Cebu. The association was established in August of 2003 and is currently supervised by initiated folks from the Concepts And Rhythm Experts Drum and Bugle Corps. CLUSTER® Drum and Bugle Corps Association is on its 19th Year of Camaraderie and Unity in the Pursuit of Excellence.
